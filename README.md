# Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed using the Sample Superstore dataset. The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product performance, and regional insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query

## Dataset

Sample Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of business performance through key metrics and visualizations.

**KPIs**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Orders

**Visualizations**

* Sales Trend Over Time
* Region vs Sales
* Category vs Sales

### 2. Product Performance

This page focuses on product-level analysis to identify high-performing products and profitable categories.

**Visualizations**

* Top 10 Products by Revenue
* Profit by Sub-Category
* Distribution of Product Categories
* Sales Contribution by Customer Segments

### 3. Customer and Regional Insights

This page provides insights into shipping preferences and state-wise profitability.

**Visualizations**

* Sales by Ship Mode
* Profit by State

## Key Insights

* Analyzed overall sales and profit performance using KPI indicators.
* Identified top-performing products based on revenue generation.
* Evaluated profitability across different product sub-categories.
* Compared sales distribution across regions and product categories.
* Examined customer segment contributions to total sales.
* Assessed shipping mode preferences and state-wise profit trends.
